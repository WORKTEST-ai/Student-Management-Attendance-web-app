# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Last Updated: December 11, 2024**

## General Questions

### What is SMA?
SMA (Student Management & Attendance) is a modern web application designed for educational institutions to manage student attendance, track academic records, and streamline administrative tasks.

### Who can use SMA?
SMA has three user roles:
- **Administrators**: Full system access
- **Teachers**: Attendance marking and class management
- **Students**: View personal attendance records

### Is SMA free to use?
SMA is open-source under the MIT License. You can use, modify, and distribute it freely.

---

## Account & Login

### How do I log in?
Enter your email and password on the login page. Credentials are provided by your school administrator.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Contact your school administrator to reset your password.

### Why can't I access certain pages?
Access is role-based. You can only access pages relevant to your role (Admin, Teacher, or Student).

---

## Attendance

### How is attendance marked?
Teachers mark attendance daily for their assigned classes. They can mark students as Present, Absent, or Late.

### Can attendance records be edited?
Only administrators can edit historical attendance records. All changes are logged in the audit system.

### How far back can I view attendance?
Students can view their entire attendance history for the current academic year.

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?

- Firefox (latest)
- Safari (latest)

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use JWT authentication, encrypted connections, and HTTP-only cookies. See our Security Policy for details.

### Does SMA work on mobile?
Yes, SMA is fully responsive and works on all devices.

## AI Features

### What AI features does SMA have?
SMA uses Google Genkit AI to analyze attendance patterns and provide insights to educators.

### Is my data used to train AI?
No. Your data is only used to generate insights, not to train AI models.
