## 5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

### 5.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **JWT Authentication**: Secure token-based authentication
- **HTTP-only Cookies**: Session cookies cannot be accessed by JavaScript
- **Role-Based Access Control**: Users can only access data relevant to their role
- **Password Protection**: Credentials are securely stored

### 5.2 Organizational Safeguards
- Access to data is limited to authorized personnel
- Regular security audits and reviews
- Staff training on data protection
- Incident response procedures

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Active user data: Retained while account is active
- Attendance records: Retained for the academic year + 5 years
- Audit logs: Retained for 2 years
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days

## 7. Children's Privacy

SMA is designed for use by educational institutions and may process data of minors (students under 18).

### 7.1 Parental Consent
- Student accounts are created by school administrators
- Parents/guardians are informed through the school's enrollment process
- Parents have the right to review their child's data

### 7.2 COPPA Compliance (For US Users)
We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act:
- We do not collect more data than necessary
- Parents can request to review or delete their child's data
- Schools act as agents for parental consent

### 7.3 GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)
For users in the European Union:
- Parental consent is required for children under 16
- Schools must ensure proper consent mechanisms
