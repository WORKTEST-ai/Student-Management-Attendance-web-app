# Data Retention Policy

**Last Updated: December 11, 2024**

## 1. Overview

This policy outlines how long SMA retains different types of data and the procedures for data deletion.

---

## 2. Retention Periods

| Data Type | Retention Period | Justification |
|-----------|------------------|---------------|
| **User Accounts** | Active + 1 year after deletion request | Account recovery, legal compliance |
| **Attendance Records** | Academic year + 5 years | Educational record requirements |
| **Audit Logs** | 2 years | Security and compliance |
| **Session Data** | 24 hours | Authentication only |
| **Temporary Files** | 7 days | Processing needs |
| **Backups** | 90 days rolling | Disaster recovery |

---

## 3. Data Categories

### 3.1 Educational Records
- Attendance data
- Class enrollments
- Academic year information
- Retained for regulatory compliance

### 3.2 User Data
- Names and emails
- Role assignments
- Login history
- Retained while account is active

### 3.3 System Data
- Audit logs
- Error logs
- Performance metrics
- Retained for operational needs

---

## 4. Deletion Procedures

### User-Initiated Deletion
1. User requests deletion via administrator
2. Administrator submits deletion request
3. Data is anonymized within 30 days
4. Backup purge after 90 days

### Automatic Deletion
- Session cookies: Auto-expire after 24 hours
- Temporary files: Cleaned weekly
- Old backups: Rotated after 90 days

### Exceptions
Data may be retained longer for:
- Legal proceedings
- Regulatory requirements
- Fraud investigation

---

## 5. Data Anonymization

When deletion is requested:
- Personal identifiers removed
- Data may remain in aggregate form
- AI training data is anonymized

---

## 6. Right to Erasure

Under GDPR and similar regulations:
- Users can request data deletion
- Schools process requests within 30 days
- Some data retained for legal compliance
